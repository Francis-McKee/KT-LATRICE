About KT LATRICE

Where Love Gets Honest

KT LATRICE is more than an artist - she is a voice rooted in truth, emotion, and authenticity. Her music blends soulful vocals with storytelling that reflects real life, real love, and real experiences.

Inspired by life's most honest moments, her sound connects deeply with listeners who value vulnerability and meaning in music. Every song carries intention - to heal, to inspire, and to speak what others feel but cannot always say.

With a growing presence and a message that resonates, KT LATRICE continues to build a space where music is not just heard, but felt.
